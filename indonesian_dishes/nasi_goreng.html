Nasi Goreng

Has anyone ever eaten fried rice? Surely almost everyone knows this one culinary. Besides being delicious, fried rice is an easy culinary to cook. There are many versions of fried rice, including kampung fried rice, green fried rice, Javanese fried rice, anchovy fried rice, seafood fried rice, fried rice, Sundanese fried rice, mawut fried rice, etc. Usually, fried rice is often served for breakfast or culinary in the evening. For Grameds who often hunt for culinary at night, Grameds can find fried rice carts whose fragrant smell can't be resisted. It is not wrong if fried rice is a culinary that has also been named the most delicious food in the world.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Protein (chicken, shrimp, or both)
  • Egg
